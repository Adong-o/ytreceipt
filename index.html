What is YouTube Receipt?

YouTube Receipt is a free tool that generates detailed analytics receipts for any YouTube channel. Simply enter a channel URL or name to get:

  • 📊 Subscriber count and total views
  • 🎥 Video statistics including Shorts
  • 📈 Upload frequency analysis
  • 🏆 Milestone achievements (1K, 100K)
  • 📅 Channel age and growth metrics

Perfect for creators, analysts, and anyone interested in YouTube channel statistics!
